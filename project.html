Visualizing Currency Exchange Rates

As part of Northwestern's CS 396 course, I learned how to generate art on the web and visualize data using HTML/CSS/JavaScript P5. For one of my projects, I chose to visualize the exchange rates of different currencies to USD. The idea is that the user can easily identify how large teh exchange rate is from that currency to USD just by looking at the word cloud and the relative size of that currency compared to others.
